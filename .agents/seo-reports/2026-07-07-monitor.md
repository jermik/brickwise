- Market index + market update routes

**Issue**: `/methodology` is absent (see HIGH finding).

`lastmod` is always `new Date()` for most routes — this is correct for a daily-scored site but means all pages show today's date regardless of content staleness. Not an error, but worth noting for future crawl-budget optimization.

Note: Both `FallbackImg` and `PropertyHeroImage` are raw `<img>` elements without `width`/`height` attributes. This is suboptimal but CLS impact is minimal where parent containers have fixed heights. The `sm:h-auto` case on the home page best-pick card is the only live CLS risk.
