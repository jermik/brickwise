### F-01 — Title over 60 characters (11 routes)

| Route | Title | Length |
|-------|-------|--------|
| `/algorand` | `Algorand Ecosystem (XX+ Projects) — DeFi, NFTs, Wallets, RWA \| Brickwise` | ~72 |
| `/compare/best-fractional-real-estate-platforms` | `Best Fractional Real Estate Platforms (2026): Honest Comparison \| Brickwise` | 75 |

| `/compare/arrived-vs-fundrise` | `Arrived vs Fundrise (2026): Single-Property vs eREIT Comparison \| Brickwise` | 75 |
| `/compare/best-real-estate-investing-apps` | `Best Real Estate Investing Apps (2026): Honest Editorial Ranking \| Brickwise` | 76 |

**Whitelist-safe**: Yes — `metadata` export in PublicShell `page.tsx` files.

### F-04 — `/methodology` absent from sitemap (HIGH severity)

`app/methodology/page.tsx` exists, uses `PublicShell`, exports `metadata` with canonical `https://brickwise.pro/methodology`, and contains SEO-valuable content (scoring weights, methodology explanation). It is **not listed** anywhere in `app/sitemap.ts`.

Googlebot discovers this page only via internal links, not via sitemap. This page is strategically important for trust and methodology transparency.

**Suggested fix**: Add to `staticRoutes` in `app/sitemap.ts`:
```ts
{ url: `${base}/methodology`, lastModified: new Date(), changeFrequency: "monthly", priority: 0.75 },
```

**Whitelist-safe**: Yes — `app/sitemap.ts` is explicitly on the whitelist.

### F-05 — Missing OG and Twitter tags on `/contact` and `/privacy`

`app/contact/page.tsx` and `app/privacy/page.tsx` have no `openGraph:` or `twitter:` blocks in their `metadata` exports. They rely entirely on inherited root-layout defaults.

This means social shares of these pages will show the generic Brickwise OG image and title, which is acceptable but suboptimal if someone shares the privacy policy or contact page directly.

| Route | Has OG? | Has Twitter? |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| `/contact` | No | No |
| `/privacy` | No | No |

**Assessment**: Low severity. These pages are not primary link targets for social sharing. The root layout fallback (type=website, image=/opengraph-image) is adequate.

**Whitelist-safe**: Yes, if flagged for fix.
