## Summary

26 PublicShell pages enumerated. All export metadata. One systemic structural bug identified: **16+ pages use plain-string titles containing `| Brickwise`** while the root layout sets `template: "%s | Brickwise"`. In Next.js App Router, this causes rendered titles to read `"... | Brickwise | Brickwise"`. This inflates effective title length and will degrade SERP display quality across most of the indexable surface. All other issues (description length overflow, a missing sitemap entry, one short privacy description) are routine maintenance items.

Sitemap and robots source reviewed — both look structurally sound. Font loading is clean (all fonts use `display: swap`, Fontshare loads non-render-blocking). No images found without dimensions.
