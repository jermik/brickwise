## Summary

The dominant issue this run is a **systematic title template bug** affecting 17 pages: plain-string `title` values that already end with `| Brickwise` get the root layout template `"%s | Brickwise"` applied, producing `"... | Brickwise | Brickwise"` in the rendered `<title>`. Most of these pages also exceed the 60-char title limit significantly as a result. Secondary issue: 10 pages have meta descriptions over 160 chars тАФ they will be truncated in SERPs.
