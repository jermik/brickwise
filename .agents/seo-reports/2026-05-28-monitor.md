## Spot-Check: Images, Layout Shift, Fonts

**Fonts:** ✅ All three Google Fonts (Inter, DM Mono, DM Serif Display) loaded via `next/font/google` with `display: "swap"`. External General Sans (fontshare CDN) uses the `media=print` non-blocking technique with `display=swap`. No issues.

**Image dimensions:** Property pages use `PropertyHeroImage` (`components/property/property-hero-image.tsx`), a `"use client"` `<img>` element without explicit `width`/`height` and no `aspect-ratio` CSS. This is a CLS risk. Algorand and city pages have no direct image elements in page markup (images inside child components).

**Layout shift hints:** No `loading="lazy"`, `fetchpriority`, or aspect-ratio wrappers on `PropertyHeroImage`. Needs human review.
