## Title Template Doubling — Affected Pages

All pages below set `title:` as a plain string containing `" | Brickwise"`. The root layout's `template: "%s | Brickwise"` wraps this again, producing `"… | Brickwise | Brickwise"` in rendered HTML.

| Route | Title string in source | Rendered length (est.) |
|---|---|---|
| `/` | `Tokenized Real Estate Scored — Lofty & RealT \| Brickwise` | 69 chars |
| `/learn/what-is-tokenized-real-estate` | `What Is Tokenized Real Estate? Complete Guide (2026) \| Brickwise` | 76 chars |
| `/learn/lofty-review` | `Lofty Review 2026: Yield, Fees, Risks & How It Works \| Brickwise` | 77 chars |
| `/learn/realt-review` | `RealT Review 2026: Yields, Fees, Risks & Is It Worth It? \| Brickwise` | 81 chars |
| `/learn/how-to-invest-in-tokenized-real-estate` | `How to Invest in Tokenized Real Estate: Step-by-Step Guide (2026) \| Brickwise` | 90 chars |
| `/algorand` | `Algorand Ecosystem (N+ Projects) — DeFi, NFTs, Wallets, RWA \| Brickwise` | ~85 chars |
| `/algorand/[slug]` | `[ProjectName] — [Category] on Algorand \| Brickwise` | varies |
| `/rankings/[category]` | `[Category title] \| Brickwise` | varies (80–90) |
| `/platform/[platform]` | `[Platform] Properties — … \| Brickwise` | varies |
| `/compare/best-fractional-real-estate-platforms` | `Best Fractional Real Estate Platforms (2026): Honest Comparison \| Brickwise` | 87 chars |
