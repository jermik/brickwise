# Brickwise Anti-Patterns

*Things we never do. Load before any public-facing change.*

## Voice anti-patterns
- Hype language anywhere on the site
- Buzzword stacking ("AI-powered platform with revolutionary insights")
- Implying affiliation with Lofty or RealT
- Implying credentialed financial advice
- Anonymous social proof ("trusted by investors")
- Inventing testimonials, customer quotes, or vanity metrics
- Comparative claims without a source link
- Em-dashes in marketing copy

## Product anti-patterns
- Adding a signup wall in front of public scoring data
- Hiding methodology behind a paywall
- Promoting a single property as a recommended buy
- Pretending "buy/hold/avoid" is advice (it is a score-derived label)
- Cluttering pages with widgets that obscure the score
- Auto-playing media
- Cookie-walling EU readers without a real reason
- Pop-ups before content load

## Content anti-patterns
- Generic crypto investing posts that any blog could publish
- "Top 10" lists with no methodology
- "How to get rich with tokenized real estate" framing
- Posts that don't name a specific property, city, or platform
- Posts without a downside section
- AI-generated filler paragraphs that pad word count
- Republishing platform marketing as analysis

## Distribution anti-patterns
- Spamming subreddits with link drops
- Posting the same copy across channels without adaptation
- Buying followers, reviews, or upvotes
- Engagement-bait questions on social
- Cold DMs to crypto influencers asking for retweets

## Strategy anti-patterns
- Pivoting to "AI for real estate" framing to chase a trend
- Adding features to compete with Lofty/RealT (we are not a marketplace)
- Pursuing enterprise/institutional surface (we are retail-facing)
- Building paid analytics tiers before the free tier has retained users
- Buying ads before organic channels are validated

## How to use this file
If a proposed change conflicts with anything here, the agent must stop and report the conflict. Owner decides whether the conflict is real or whether the anti-pattern needs revision.
