# Brickwise Positioning

*Authoritative. Load this before any agent runs.*

## One-liner
Independent yield, risk, and fair-value scoring for every tokenized rental property on Lofty and RealT.

## What we are
- Independent third-party analytics for tokenized real estate
- A content site + free analytics tool, public-facing
- Numerate, methodology-transparent, skeptical by default
- A scoreboard, not a marketplace

## What we are NOT
- Not affiliated with Lofty or RealT
- Not a brokerage
- Not a generic AI growth platform
- Not a crypto hype site
- Not an enterprise dashboard
- Not Cleveland-specific (one property is in Cleveland; the site is national in scope)
- Not a token-flipping signal service
- Not a financial advisor

## Moat (compounds over time)
1. Proprietary longitudinal market data across the entire tokenized-rental universe
2. Normalized cross-platform tracking (only third-party doing this for Lofty + RealT)
3. Historical change data (score drift, yield trends, occupancy changes)
4. Public trust around methodology transparency and independence

## Target audience
- Crypto-curious retail investors holding ETH/USDC, looking for RWA yield
- Yield-focused fractional-RE investors comparing tokenized vs incumbents
- Data-driven passive investors wanting transparent scoring before deploying capital

## Anti-personas
- Institutional buyers needing offer-memo-grade due diligence
- Pure-REIT investors who refuse to touch a crypto wallet
- Short-term token flippers or speculators chasing price action

## Conversion hierarchy
1. Email capture (primary near-term)
2. Affiliate click to Lofty / RealT / Ark7 / Fundrise / Arrived (revenue)
3. Return visits (organic SEO compounding)

## What "good" looks like
A skeptical retail investor lands on a property page, reads the score, sees the methodology, agrees with the "avoid" call on a 30.9% yield trap, subscribes to the newsletter, and tells one friend. They never feel sold to.
