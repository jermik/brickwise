# FactuurDirect Growth Model

## Current mode
Product-rebrand mode active per `project_factuurdirect_product_mode` memory. SEO is frozen; product/UX is the focus. Five sequential phases: visual → onboarding/VAT → workflows → supporting features → pricing.

## Channels (ranked, post-rebrand)
1. **Onboarding activation** — get more signups to a sent first invoice
2. **Word of mouth and ZZP communities** — slow but real
3. **Organic SEO** for Dutch ZZP-facing queries (paused until product is stable)
4. **Comparison content** — calm and factual against Moneybird and Exact (post-rebrand)
5. **Bookkeeper partnerships** — light, no commission-heavy programs

## Channels we are NOT using
- Paid ads until activation is healthy
- Cold email to scraped KVK lists
- Influencer or affiliate stunts
- LinkedIn enterprise outreach

## Conversion ladder
1. Signup
2. First invoice sent (activation)
3. Second invoice within 14 days (retention)
4. Plan choice
5. Renewal

## Content shape that works
- Plain Dutch explainers for common ZZP tax situations
- Migration guides from competitors
- Onboarding walkthroughs

## Content shape that does NOT work
- "AI for invoicing" trend pieces
- Generic "10 tips for freelancers" filler
- Comparison content that puts competitors down
