# FactuurDirect Tone

## Voice
Calm. Trustworthy. Practical. Approachable. Predictable.

## Anchor sentence (Dutch)
> "Klaar. Je factuur is verstuurd."

In English: "Done. Your invoice is sent." That cadence is the brand.

## Language
- Dutch first for product copy and SMB outreach
- English allowed for developer-facing copy if needed
- Per owner preference: no em-dashes anywhere

## Sentence rules
- Short. Calm. Confident.
- Lead with what just happened or what to do next
- Avoid celebratory exclamation marks
- Use present tense for system status
- Explain BTW or tax decisions in one line, near where they happen

## Words to use (NL)
factuur, klant, betaling, BTW, overzicht, klaar, versturen, kopie, herinnering, status, eenvoudig, duidelijk, voorspelbaar

## Words to avoid
- "Powered by AI"
- "Slim" or "Smart" used as adjectives for automation
- "Disruptie"
- "Game-changer"
- "Revolutionair"
- "Volgende generatie"
- Excessive exclamation marks
- Em-dashes

## Tone tests
1. **Calm test** — would a stressed ZZP'er feel slower-heartbeat after reading this?
2. **Predictable test** — does the copy tell the user exactly what will happen next?
3. **No-AI-theatre test** — is automation explained, not hidden behind "smart" language?

## Explicitly NOT
Excited, salesy, urgent, gamified, AI-magic, enterprise-formal, finfluencer.
