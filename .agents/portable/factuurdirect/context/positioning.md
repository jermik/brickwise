# FactuurDirect Positioning

## One-liner
Calm Dutch-first invoicing that lets a freelancer or ZZP'er finish their administration without thinking about it.

## What we are
- Calm workflow software for simple administration
- Dutch-first language and tax model (BTW)
- Onboarding-speed obsessed
- Stress-reducing by design
- Mobile-first for invoicing on the go

## What we are NOT
- Not an ERP
- Not an enterprise accounting suite
- Not an "AI bookkeeping autopilot"
- Not feature-maximizing finance software
- Not a generalist SaaS

## Moat
1. Onboarding to first invoice measured in minutes, not hours
2. Dutch tax model native (BTW, KOR, reverse charge, ICP) without configuration
3. Calm, predictable UX with no hidden automation
4. Price point and feature shape sized for ZZP and small SMB

## Target audience
- ZZP'ers (freelancers) in NL
- One-to-five-person SMBs sending invoices monthly
- Bookkeepers helping small clients lightly

## Anti-personas
- Multi-entity corporate finance teams
- Accountants who want full ledger control (refer them to Moneybird Pro / Exact)
- Users who want bank reconciliation as the core workflow

## Conversion hierarchy
1. Signup completed and first invoice sent
2. Second invoice within 14 days (activation)
3. Plan upgrade or annual renewal

## What "good" looks like
A new ZZP'er signs up on their phone, completes onboarding in three minutes, sends their first invoice in seven, and never has to look up "hoe BTW verleggen" because the product set it correctly without asking.
