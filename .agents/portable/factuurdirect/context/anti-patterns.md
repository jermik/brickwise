# FactuurDirect Anti-Patterns

## Voice anti-patterns
- "AI bookkeeping" framing
- "Smart automation" used to hide what the product is doing
- Celebratory tone after every action ("Yay! Invoice sent!")
- Urgency or scarcity in app UI
- English copy in primary user flows
- Em-dashes

## Product anti-patterns
- Adding ERP-shaped features (purchase orders, multi-entity, multi-currency consolidation)
- Auto-categorization that the user cannot see or override
- Hidden automation steps the user cannot replay or undo
- Modal pop-ups during invoice creation
- Streak counters, badges, gamification
- "AI assistant" chat in the invoice flow
- Bank reconciliation as the core workflow (it is a future supporting feature, not the hero)
- Pricing tiers that gate basic invoicing behind premium

## Onboarding anti-patterns
- Setup wizards longer than three steps
- Asking for VAT/KVK numbers before the user has invested time
- Sample data that confuses real first-use
- Welcome emails with feature tours instead of "send your first invoice" CTA

## Growth anti-patterns
- Cold email blasts to scraped KVK lists
- Affiliate programs that require complex tracking
- Comparison content that disparages Moneybird or Exact
- AI-content blog spam in Dutch

## Strategy anti-patterns
- Adding a feature because a competitor has it
- Pursuing accountants as the primary buyer (we are end-user first)
- Pricing increases without notice
- Removing free-tier capabilities customers have come to expect
