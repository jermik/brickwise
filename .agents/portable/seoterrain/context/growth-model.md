# SEO Terrain Growth Model

## Channels (ranked)
1. **Free audit funnel** — the audit is the lead magnet
2. **Local SEO landing pages** — service + city combos
3. **Organic SEO** for SMB-shaped queries ("local SEO audit for plumbers")
4. **Partner referrals** — agencies serving SMBs that need audits at scale
5. **Directory submissions** — SaaS and SMB tool directories for backlinks

## Channels we are NOT using
- Paid ads until the audit-to-paid funnel is validated
- SEO Twitter influencer marketing
- Cold email to scraped lists
- LinkedIn enterprise outreach

## Conversion ladder
1. Owner searches for "why am I not ranking" or sees a local SEO landing page
2. Requests free audit (URL + email)
3. Receives audit report
4. Books consult or buys a plan
5. Refers other SMB owners

## Content shape that works
- "Why [type of business] in [city] aren't ranking" teardowns
- Before/after audit case studies (with permission)
- Specific-fix tutorials ("how to rewrite your homepage title for local SEO")
- Local-pack and Google Business Profile explainers

## Content shape that does NOT work
- Generic "what is SEO" 101 posts
- AI tool comparison content
- "Top 10 SEO trends" annual filler
- Enterprise SEO case studies
