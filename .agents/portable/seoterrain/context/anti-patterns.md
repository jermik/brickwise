# SEO Terrain Anti-Patterns

## Voice anti-patterns
- "AI-powered SEO" framing anywhere
- Faking competitor data
- Vanity report metrics with no action
- Cold-traffic urgency ("act now or lose rankings")
- Anonymous "trusted by 10,000 businesses" social proof
- Em-dashes in marketing copy

## Product anti-patterns
- Building a generic SEO dashboard
- Adding crawler features that compete with Ahrefs/Semrush
- Becoming an agency platform
- Adding AI content generation as a feature
- Auto-generating doorway pages from audit data
- Hiding the actual fixes behind an upgrade wall

## Audit-report anti-patterns
- Generic "improve your meta descriptions" without showing the meta
- Score-only reports with no action
- Issues lists without prioritization
- Reports that require an SEO degree to read
- Reports longer than the SMB will read (cap reasonable)

## Distribution anti-patterns
- Buying audit-tool review placements
- Spamming local-biz subreddits or Facebook groups
- Cold-emailing scraped lists of local businesses
- Mimicking influencer agency marketing
