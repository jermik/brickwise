# SEO Terrain Positioning

## One-liner
Local SEO audits and competitor gap reports that an SMB owner can read in five minutes and act on this week.

## What we are
- An audit-first lead-generation product for local businesses
- Practical, grounded, usefulness over flash
- Trust-driven, no jargon, no enterprise theater
- SMB-shaped (one-owner, one-shop)

## What we are NOT
- Not a giant SEO suite (Ahrefs / Semrush / Moz)
- Not an AI SEO spam tool that auto-generates pages
- Not an enterprise agency platform
- Not yet another generic SEO utility graveyard

## Moat
1. Audit reports designed for SMB readability, not analyst spreadsheets
2. Local competitor gap analysis tied to specific recommendations
3. Lead-routing built into the audit funnel
4. Practical fix language, not "optimize your meta tags"

## Target audience
- Local service businesses (plumbers, dentists, lawyers, contractors, gyms)
- Small agencies serving those businesses
- Owner-operators who Google "why am I not on Google maps"

## Anti-personas
- Enterprise SEO teams
- Agencies running 100+ clients on Ahrefs
- Pure-affiliate SEO marketers building doorway sites

## Conversion hierarchy
1. Audit request submitted
2. Audit report delivered
3. Follow-up consult or upgrade

## What "good" looks like
A local plumber requests an audit, gets a five-page report that names the three competitors outranking them and the two specific things to fix this week, books a consult, and tells their accountant about it.
